- Create an `inbound_pitches` table with columns: `id`, `sender_id` (Silver user), `receiver_id` (Gold user), `project_name`, `pitch_text` (max 280 chars), `deck_url`, `status` (pending, accepted, declined), and `created_at`.

- Inside the settings or profile area for Gold members, build a minimalist "Inbound Pitches" dashboard.

- Whenever a new pitch is inserted into the `inbound_pitches` table, trigger an automated email notification to the Gold user's registered email address.
- The email must cleanly present: The Project Name, the Pitch Text, the Deck Link, and a call-to-action button linking directly back to their "Inbound Pitches" dashboard.
